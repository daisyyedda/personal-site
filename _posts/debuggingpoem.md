It’s me.

Buggy bunny bug.

A name I refer to myself.

A name self explanatory and representative.

A name embedded with persona manifestation.

--

Desperation.

Disguised.

Melancholy.

Intertwined.

--

Anticlimax.

Period.

--

It’s me.

Buggy bunny bug.

Striving to debug my life.
